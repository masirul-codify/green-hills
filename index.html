Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q. How far is Green Hills City Armenia from Yerevan?

    Green Hills City Armenia is 15 minutes away from Yerevan and is accessible via the M1 highway from the road leading to Yeghvard. The journey can take up to 30 minutes, depending on traffic. Green Hills City Armenia is an ideal place for nature lovers who want to explore rural Armenia and get away from the hustle and bustle of Yerevan city life and yet be minutes awat from the Capital.

  • Q. How far is Green Hills City Armenia from the Airport?

    Green Hills City Armenia is approximately 20 km (10.5 miles) away from Zvartnots International Airport, which is about a 20 minute drive via the North-West Expressway.

  • Q. Green hills City Delivery Date ?

    The Green Hills project is expected to be completed in five phases.

    Phase 1 will include the construction of the infrastructure, the venue and the project entrance. We will provide The Water, Electricity , Gas And around 20% of the total project area will be ready for development of residential homes by summer 2024.

    Phase 2 will include the construction of the infrastructure for another 20% of the Project infrastructure, The Lake and the Gym with Additional Green Areas will be as well delivered. Phase 2 delivery is expected in 2025.

    Phase 3 will include the construction of the infrastructure for another 20% of the Project infrastructure, The second Lake will be executed Phase 3 delivery is expected in 2026.

    Phase 4 will include the construction of the infrastructure for another 20% of the Project , The bus station, nursery and school. Phase 4 delivery is expected in 2027.

    Phase 5 will include the construction of the infrastructure for another 20% of the Project infrastructure, and the delivery for all the common areas of the project. Phase 5 delivery is expected in 2028.

  • Q. How big is Green Hills City?

    The total area of Green Hills City Armenia is estimated at around 100 Ha. The small but picturesque city will have plenty of opportunities to explore and enjoy. There will be parks, gardens, riverside trails, a lake, meadows and forests, as well as a variety of sports grounds and leisure activities. The city planners are keen to ensure that the development of this city would be in strict adherence to environmental regulations and sustainability principles.

    The first phase of the project is expected to be completed by 2023-2024, with all five phases being completed by 2028.

  • Q. How Many families will live at Green Hills City?

    Green Hills City Armenia is set to become home to around 400 families upon completion of the five-phase development project. Each phase will include the construction of Private homes, semi-detached homes, parks, gardens, riverside trails, a lake, meadows and forests, as well as a variety of sports grounds and leisure activities.

  • Q. Approximately , what are the maintenance charges per Home at Green Hills City ?

    The maintenance charges per Home at Green Hills City are expected to be quite competitive, with a variety of services available for families, starting from trash removal, snow plowing and lawn care can expect to pay around 50,000 AMD per month. Those seeking more comprehensive services, including regular property inspections and emergency repairs, can expect to pay up to 70,000 per month.

  • Q. Approximately , what are the maintenance charges for a non-built Land at Green Hills City ?

    The maintenance charges for an unbuilt land at Green Hills City Armenia will vary depending on the size of the land and the services required. For example, those who purchase smaller plots (less than 700 sq m) may expect to pay around 15,000 Dram per month in maintenance costs. This fee covers basic services such as grass cutting, hedge trimming repairs and public amenities cost.

  • Q. When should land and home buyers expect to start paying for the Maintenance Fees ?

    Home and land buyers at Green Hills City Armenia can expect to start paying for maintenance fees once the project is completed in 2028.
